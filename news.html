Getting started

TRON energy rental lets you rent network energy without staking TRX. You pay a small fee per kilosecond of energy, use it for transactions (USDT transfers, DEX swaps, etc.), and the energy is released when the rental period ends. It's a flexible, cost-effective alternative to locking up TRX.
We aggregate prices from 19+ energy rental platforms in real time. Each platform is checked at regular intervals for their 65k and 130k 1-hour rental rates. The board updates automatically and always shows the cheapest option first, so you can pick the best deal instantly.
Some platforms offer discounted rates through referral links. Cards highlighted with a blue "Get Energy" button have active referral deals — clicking through may give you a better price on your first rental. Green-highlighted cards in the old design marked these; now look for the blue referral button.
Prices are fetched from each platform every few minutes. The "updated —" badge in the header shows when the last successful refresh happened. Platforms that haven't reported in over 24 hours are shown as stale (dimmed) to avoid misleading data.
